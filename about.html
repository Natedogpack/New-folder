About

A full service provider serving Franklin, Adams, Fulton, and Cumberland Counties in Pennsylvania
Washington County in Maryland. David offer tuning and general repairs for the home, church,
institution, and concert stage pianos you hold dear. Authorized sales and installer for Piano Life Saver
humidity control systems.

A graduate of the Randy Potter School of Piano Technology, David is an active member of the Piano
Technicians Guild. Born and raised in Southern California, David completed a highly successful 24 year
career in the United States Navy where he was privilaged to visit over thirty countries around the world.
He graduated with a B.A. in Music from (Pasadena College) Point Loma College, San Diego, CA. David
has been playing the piano & organ in churches for over 45 years and teaches piano & voice at the
Cumberland Valley School of Music
